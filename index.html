❓ Common Doubts — Answered

Customers ask these every day. Here are clear answers.

Revenue certificates (Income, Caste, Residence) typically take 7 to 15 working days after submission. The Tahsildar must verify your details before approving. We will SMS you the transaction ID — you can track the status at ts.meeseva.telangana.gov.in.
Bring Original documents AND 1 photocopy of each. Our operator will scan the originals. For Aadhaar enrolment, originals only (no photocopies needed). Never submit originals without getting an acknowledgment slip.
Yes! We do all Aadhaar services — new enrolment (FREE), name/address/DOB correction (₹50 per update), and mobile number update (requires biometric, ₹50). Bring your Aadhaar number and the supporting document for your change.
If birth was not registered within 21 days, it is a Late Registration. This requires a Non-Availability Certificate from your Panchayat/Municipality + a notarized affidavit + hospital records. It takes 15–30 days. Come to our centre first and we'll guide you through it.
Yes. Certificates issued through MeeSeva carry a unique QR code and digital signature from the Telangana Government. They are legally valid at all government offices, banks, colleges and courts across India.
Income Certificate is valid for 1 year only from the date of issue. You must renew it every year if you need it for scholarships, Kalyana Lakshmi, EWS, or other welfare schemes.
